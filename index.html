Welcome from Our Headteacher

At Webitor Primary School, we are committed to providing an outstanding education for all our pupils. Our dedicated staff work tirelessly to ensure every child reaches their full potential in a safe, nurturing environment.

We believe in developing the whole child - academically, socially, and emotionally. Our broad and balanced curriculum is designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Mrs. Sarah Thompson
Headteacher
